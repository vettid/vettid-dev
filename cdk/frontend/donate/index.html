Support VettID

VettID is a member-owned cooperative committed to protecting digital privacy. Your donation helps cover hosting, development, and infrastructure costs. Every satoshi goes directly to keeping VettID running and improving.

Where Your Donation Goes

  • AWS Infrastructure - Nitro Enclave instances, DynamoDB, CloudFront, and other cloud services
  • Development - Ongoing security improvements, new features, and bug fixes
  • Security Audits - Third-party security reviews to ensure your data stays safe
  • Domain & Certificates - Keeping vettid.dev and vettid.org secure and accessible
